Why is it important to protect Earth?

"The LORD God took the man and put him in the garden of Eden to work it and keep it." - Genesis 2:15, ESV

The Hebrew words translated “work” and “take care of” in Genesis 2:15 may also be translated “serve” and “preserve.”

We here at Conserving Creation are passionate about being good stewards of the gifts God gave us here on earth. We have conservation efforts happening all over the world thanks to the contributions from people like you.

Some projects you can contribute to are:

  • Beach Clean-Up
  • Road-Side Clean-Up
  • Animal Habitat Restoration
  • Endangered Species Studies
  • Recycling Awareness Program
  • Water Conservation Program
  • Bringing Sustainable Power to Rural Areas


If you are also passionate about protecting our planet please consider donating your time or financially contributing to our cause.

About

Conserving Creation is a non-profit dedicated to protecting the resources God gave us. All funds raised go to habitat restoration, disaster clean up, and other restorative projects that help conserve the environment.
